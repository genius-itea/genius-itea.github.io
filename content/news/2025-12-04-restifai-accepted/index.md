It's time for a samba class!
Our Paper "RESTifAI: LLM-Based Workflow for Reusable REST API Testing" has been accepted for the ICSE 2026 Demonstration Track.
Incredibly proud of the team **Leon Kogler**, **Benedikt Dornauer** (**Universität Innsbruck**), **Martin Hairer** and the entire development team at **CASABLANCA hotelsoftware GmbH**.
A special thanks to our coauthor **Eduard Enoiu**, to **Peter Schrammel** for giving us valueable feedback and to **GENIUS**.
Looking forward to present the work in Rio.
If you’d like to learn more, we’ve put together a [short blog post](https://research.casablanca.at/post/restifai-a-prototype-for-smarter-api-testing) with additional details.
